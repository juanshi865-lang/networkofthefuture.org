The Conference About NoF

The International Conference on Network of the Future (NoF) is a premier annual forum dedicated to future internet architectures, network softwarization, and intelligent communication systems. Since its inception in 2010, NoF has brought together researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to present and discuss innovative ideas shaping the future of networking.

Each edition features a Best Paper Award, selected by the Technical Program Committee based on originality, technical quality, and potential impact. Technically co-sponsored by the IEEE Communications Society, all accepted and presented papers are published in IEEE Xplore.
