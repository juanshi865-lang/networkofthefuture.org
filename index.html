NoF 2024 Best Paper Award Announced

The Organizing Committee of the 15th International Conference on Network of the Future (NoF 2024) is pleased to announce the winners of the NoF 2024 Best Paper Award.

🏆 Best Paper Award — NoF 2024
Predictive Resource Orchestration in Softwarized Networks: A Digital Twin Approach
Authors: José Cunha and Susana Sargento

Award Details

About the Winning Paper

The paper presents a novel digital twin framework for predictive resource orchestration in softwarized network environments. By creating a continuously synchronized virtual replica of the physical network infrastructure, the proposed system enables real-time what-if analysis and proactive resource allocation decisions.

The authors demonstrate through extensive simulations that the digital twin approach reduces service-level agreement violations by up to 34% and improves resource utilization efficiency by 28% compared to reactive orchestration strategies, while maintaining computational overhead within practical limits for operational deployment.

Selection Process

The Best Paper Award was selected by the NoF 2024 Technical Program Committee. Two outstanding papers of equal merit were recognized. Papers were evaluated on originality, technical quality, clarity of presentation, and potential impact on future internet research. The award was presented during the closing session on 4 October 2024.

About NoF 2024

The 15th edition of the International Conference on Network of the Future was held in Castelldefels/Barcelona, Spain, from 2 to 4 October 2024. Organized by DNAC, France, the conference featured technical sessions, keynote speeches, and the Best Paper Award ceremony. The proceedings are published in IEEE Xplore.
