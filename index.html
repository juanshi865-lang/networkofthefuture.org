Conference Profile

International Conference
on Network of the Future

The International Conference on Network of the Future (NoF) is a premier annual venue focused on emerging network paradigms—from softwarized infrastructures and programmable data planes to AI-driven orchestration and edge-native services. Now in its 15th edition, NoF serves as a bridge between academic research and real-world network transformation.

The Best Paper Award recognizes work that demonstrates both technical excellence and the potential to influence practical network deployments. Selection is conducted by the full Technical Program Committee through single-blind review.

“The NoF Best Paper Award reflects our commitment to research that not only advances theory but also charts a credible path toward deployment in operational networks. Both winning papers exemplify this balance.”

— NoF 2024 Technical Program Committee
