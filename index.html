The ConferenceAbout NoF

The International Conference on Network of the Future (NoF) is a premier annual forum dedicated to future internet architectures, network softwarisation, and intelligent communication systems. Since its inception in 2010, NoF has brought together researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to present and discuss innovative ideas shaping the future of networking.

Each edition features a Best Paper Award, selected by the Technical Program Committee based on originality, technical quality, and potential impact. Technically co-sponsored by the IEEE Communications Society, all accepted and presented papers are published in IEEE Xplore.

“Two papers were recognised this year for their exceptional contributions to the field—one advancing digital twin orchestration in softwarised networks, the other tackling latency-critical traffic steering in next-generation content delivery architectures.” — NoF 2024 Technical Program Committee
