News & Announcements

Updates on the NoF 2024 Best Paper Award recipients and conference highlights.

15 October 2024

NoF 2024 Best Paper Award — Yinan Qian

Yinan Qian receives the Best Paper Award for research on adaptive edge-to-cloud traffic steering. The paper introduces a novel framework for latency-critical CDN applications, validated with production traffic traces. Read more →

15 October 2024

NoF 2024 Best Paper Award — José Cunha & Susana Sargento

José Cunha and Susana Sargento are recognized for their work on digital twin-based predictive resource orchestration in softwarized networks, demonstrating significant improvements in SLA compliance and resource efficiency. Read more →

2–4 October 2024

NoF 2024 Held Successfully in Castelldefels, Spain

The 15th edition of the International Conference on Network of the Future was held in Castelldefels/Barcelona, Spain, featuring technical sessions, invited talks, and the Best Paper Award ceremony.
