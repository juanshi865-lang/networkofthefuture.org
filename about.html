About the NoF
Best Paper Award

The NoF Best Paper Award is an annual recognition presented at the International Conference on Network of the Future (NoF) to honour outstanding contributions to the field of future internet research and network innovation.

Purpose

The award recognises exceptional papers that demonstrate originality, technical rigour, and the potential to significantly impact the evolution of network architectures, protocols, and services. It is a hallmark of excellence in the NoF community.

Selection Process

All accepted papers undergo single-blind peer review. The Best Paper Award is selected by the Technical Program Committee from among the accepted and presented papers. Evaluation criteria include:

  • Originality and novelty of the contribution
  • Technical quality and soundness of the methodology
  • Clarity and quality of presentation
  • Potential impact on future internet research and industry practice
“The Best Paper Award reflects the highest standard of research excellence within the NoF community, chosen by a rigorous committee process that values both technical depth and visionary thinking.” — NoF Technical Program Committee

Technical Sponsorship

NoF is technically co-sponsored by the IEEE Communications Society. All accepted and presented papers are published in IEEE Xplore, ensuring worldwide visibility and archival preservation.

Scope of Research

The conference invites original contributions in future internet design, including but not limited to: network softwarisation (SDN/NFV), network slicing, edge/fog computing, cloud and data center networks, content delivery architectures, network resilience, network automation and orchestration, network monitoring and analytics, and intelligent network systems powered by machine learning.
